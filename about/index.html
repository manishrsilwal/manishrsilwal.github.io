About

I'm Manish. I design digital products for a living and I've been at it for over a decade. I live in Lalitpur, Nepal and work with teams all over the world.

I studied Computer Science at Kathmandu University, which means I can talk to developers without a translator. That background shows up in everything I design. I think about what's buildable, not just what looks good in Figma.

Most of my work has been in ed-tech, where I've designed for products used by students, teachers, parents, and school leaders all at once. Different people, different needs, same product. That kind of complexity is what I enjoy most.

In 2025, I co-founded Jungle Tech, a small studio where I get to do everything I care about: strategy, design, building, and talking to the people who'll actually use what we make.
