Kung Fu Quiz

A gamified quiz platform that turns boring assessments into something students actually want to do.

Role Senior Product Designer
Company Innovate Tech
Timeline 2021 — 2022
Type Product Design — Desktop, Tablet & Mobile

The Problem

Quizzes are one of the most dreaded parts of school. Students treat them as chores. Kung Fu Quiz was supposed to change that by making assessments genuinely engaging, without turning them into mindless games.

What I Did

I designed a progression system around a martial arts theme. Students earn belts as they master topics, which gives them a clear sense of where they stand. The metaphor works because it’s playful without being patronizing.

The real challenge was the real-time competitive mode. Two students, a countdown timer, live scoring, and instant feedback. On a phone screen, that’s a lot of information fighting for space. I spent most of my time figuring out what could go and what absolutely had to stay.

On the teacher side, I kept things straightforward. Create a quiz, pick from a question bank, assign it to a class, check results. No learning curve.

The Result

Students engaged with quizzes far more than they did with traditional formats. The competitive element made assessments something students actually started on their own, without being told to.
