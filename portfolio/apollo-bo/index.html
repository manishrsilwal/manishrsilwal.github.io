APollo BO

The backoffice that powers mySecondTeacher. Content management, user admin, and analytics in one dashboard.

Role Senior Product Designer
Company Innovate Tech
Timeline 2021 — 2023
Type Product Design — Desktop

The Problem

mySecondTeacher’s content team was growing, and they needed a proper backoffice. Managing courses, tracking student activity, handling accounts, and reviewing analytics were all happening through workarounds. They needed one tool that could do all of it without requiring technical skills.

What I Did

I built the content management workflow first because that’s what people used most. Uploading a lesson, attaching a video, and hitting publish needed to feel as easy as writing an email.

The dashboard landing page shows three things: active students, content gaps, and engagement trends. I deliberately left out the 20 other metrics that the team thought they wanted. If a number doesn’t change how you act, it doesn’t belong on the first screen.

I also designed role-based access so admins, content managers, and school coordinators each see only what’s relevant to their job.

The Result

The backoffice became the operational core of mySecondTeacher. Content managers could publish lessons on their own without waiting for a developer, which removed the biggest bottleneck in the content pipeline.
